Platform Features

SkillMatch AI offers powerful features for job seekers, employers, and administrators to streamline the hiring process.

For Job Seekers

Create detailed skill profiles, update portfolios, and receive AI-matched job recommendations based on your actual abilities.

For Employers

Post jobs with required skills and review AI-matched candidates that truly fit your needs, saving time and resources.

AI-Driven Matching

Our advanced algorithms analyze skills, experience, and potential to create perfect matches beyond what traditional methods can achieve.

AI Chat Assistant

Recruiters can query the candidate database using natural language to find the perfect match for their requirements.

Interview Management

Schedule and manage interviews with automated notifications and reminders for a seamless hiring process.

Career Insights

Get AI-recommended career paths based on your skills, industry trends, and potential growth opportunities.
